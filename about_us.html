About Us

"Roshan Safar" is dedicated to the social welfare and in the interest of the less privileged and the destitute. We aim to reinvigorate a concept of social upbringing amongst the masses that is absolutely essential if we are to move forward as a nation, as one.

With our primary objective being focused on increasing the literacy rate for Pakistan, the elimination of child labor, and the economic uplift od women; particularly with reference to honor killings, acid violence and provision of employment opportunities to end gender discrimination. Extending close cooperation to National and International donors and agencies.
